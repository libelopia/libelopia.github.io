Libelopia is an animation studio founded in 2020 by Laura Pop,
specialising in art direction, 2D animation, and animation workshops.

We produce short films for NGOs, film festivals, musicians, we develop our own animated shorts,
and we provide mentorship for children and adults.


We like to capture the complexity in simple things and the multitude of perspectives that surround us.
